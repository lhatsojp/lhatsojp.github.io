My NYC Landmark: Cofetaria Nita(Nita's European Bakery)

Background

Cofetaria Nita was a local bakery by my old home in Sunnyside, Queens. Located behind my apartment building amd next to the local park, this bakery brings me back to my childhood memories. From my proucpine birthday cake to hot summer days walking through my neighborhood, this small buisness represents the resilience of immigrants in New York as they recreate the homes they once knew.

On this website you can view my essay on this bakery as well as some pictures of the treats inside the bakery! If you would like to visit, I also included its location in a Google Map. Lastly, there is a About the Author page if you would like to get to know me better! Next to this, is a short video of the bakery from another point of view.
